Step into the future of AI-Native Workflows.

Agentic Workflow Architecture (AWA) is the industry standard for designing, querying, and visualizing processes meant for AI Agents and human collaboration.

Why AWA?

Traditional BPMN was built for humans and brittle software systems. In the era of LLMs and autonomous agents, we need a specification that is queryable, context-aware, and outcome-focused.

Hello World

Creating your first Agentic Workflow takes less than 20 lines of code.

import { workflow } from '@awa/sdk';

const helloAWA = workflow('Greeting Process', '1.0.0')
  .description('A simple AI greeting flow')
  .activity('Generate Greeting', {
    actor_type: 'ai_agent',
    role_id: 'copywriter',
    procedure: 'Greet the user in a friendly manner'
  })
  .edge('START', 'Generate Greeting')
  .build();

console.log(JSON.stringify(helloAWA, null, 2));
